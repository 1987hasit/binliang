Who am I?

Highly focused and analytical researcher with a strong background in data mining, machine learning, and computer vision. Conduct research and produce papers at top-tier conferences and journals (TIP, CIKM, ICARCV, ECCV, and ICCV). Mentor and co-supervise PhD students. Years of experience in using predictive modelling, data processing, and data mining algorithms to solve business problems. Drive and participate projects with Australian water utilities.

Obtained the PhD degree in Computer Science from Charles Sturt University in 2016. Did research on Human Gesture Recognition with Depth Camera at School of Computing and Mathematics under the guidence of Dr. Lihong Zheng. Obtained the Master's degree in Computer Software and Theory in July 2012 and the Bachlor's degree in Computer Science and Technology in July 2009, both from Taiyuan University of Technology.
